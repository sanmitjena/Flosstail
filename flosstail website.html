About Us

The Story Behind
FlossTail

Born from a simple question: why can't celebrating feel as light, fun, and instant as cotton candy?

Our Story

Instant joy,
engineered for everyone

FlossTail started as an MBA entrepreneurship project at Srusti Academy of Management & Technology, Bhubaneswar — but the idea behind it is universal.

We noticed that people constantly crave a fun, celebratory drink — but existing options were either too expensive, too bulky, or not portable. Premium mocktails were trapped behind café counters. Cotton candy melted in minutes.

So we invented FlossTail — a nitrogen-sealed cotton candy pouch that transforms into a fresh mocktail in 30 seconds. No refrigeration. No power. No fuss. Just celebration — wherever you are.

Strategic Analysis

FlossTail SWOT Analysis

S

💪 Strength

Unique, portable, affordable mocktail with long shelf life. No competitor combines cotton candy aesthetics with a functional mocktail experience.

W

⚠️ Weakness

As a new concept, FlossTail may face low consumer awareness initially and sensitivity to humidity in extreme conditions.

O

🚀 Opportunity

Growing demand for innovative RTD beverages among young consumers. India's RTD market is growing at 18% CAGR.

T

⚡ Threat

Established beverage brands could replicate the concept. Ultra-low-sugar preference shifts may need product adaptation.
